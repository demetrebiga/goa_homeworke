Chevrolet Camaro

The Chevrolet Camaro is a mid-size American automobile manufactured by Chevrolet, classified as a pony car.
It first went on sale on September 29, 1966,
for the 1967 model year and was designed to compete with the Ford Mustang.



Four distinct generations of the Camaro were developed before production ended in 2002.
The nameplate was revived on a concept car that evolved into the fifth-generation Camaro; production started on March 16, 2009.
